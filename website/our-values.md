# Our core values

Anyone who participates in a CODA space or works on a CODA task is a CODA member, and we aim to collaborate with projects that share our values. By participating, you agree to uphold the spirit of the [Ubuntu code of conduct](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/ubuntu-code-of-conduct-v2-0/33111/1), champion the CODA values described below, and help set the standard for open-source collaboration.

**Transparency**  
Our work is conducted openly. Documentation, workflows, and community platforms are freely available to anyone seeking to improve documentation.

**Diversity**  
We welcome and celebrate contributors from all backgrounds and geographies. Participation is encouraged regardless of prior expertise or origin.

**Partnership**  
We thrive through collective effort. Through facilitation, peer assistance, and shared knowledge, we strengthen each other's capabilities in a respectful and growth-oriented community.

**Quality**  
We pursue industry-leading documentation standards, supporting contributors at their current level and facilitating professional development through experience in established projects.
